About

Hey I'm Boateng Dickson.

A dedicated frontend developer and technical writer now based in Ghana's capital, Accra.


I have always been a fan of computers and how they operate...

I discovered my passion for coding nearly three years ago when I started making my own static web pages in HTML and CSS. Since then I have been constantly improving my skills and learning new languages and technologies.


Right now I am mostly practicing and making personal projects in HTML/CSS, Javascript and React. I am hoping to set foot in the industry soon and start working for a company where I can add new tools to my skillset, improve the existing ones and grow as a part of a team of professional programmers.


Now, I want to help others find joy in coding too...

Unemployment is at an all-time high even among college grads from where I live. I believe the problem is that most students attend university with the sole aim of being employed by the government afterwards, only to discover that this is not the case. As a result, a significant number of people are simply just waiting on the government to recruit them.


This, in my opinion, is absurd. So, in my own small way, I try to educate some of these people about programming in order to encourage them to pursue careers in technology. In addition, I publish beginner-friendly programming articles to assist them in making the move into technology so that they can become self-employed with the acquired skills and not rely on the government.


I feel that expecting the government to come and save you is the quickest way to become destitute.


If you want to know me personally

When I'm not programming, I like to spend my spare time writing blog posts, walking around the neighbourhood, doing some graphic design works and hanging out with family and friends.
