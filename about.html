MY JOURNEY TO WEB DEVELOPMENT

My web development journey began in early 2020, amid the epidemic. I began exploring several domains to see which would be the greatest fit for me. I started by learning Python and took a few courses on Coursera, but after discovering that web programming was my true passion, I switched to JavaScript.


I took a handful of online courses which focused on both fundamentals and advanced topics in web development. After that, a software engineering friend advised me to get active on Tech Twitter. I believe it was the finest advise I ever received since through Twitter, I was introduced to a wealth of valuable web development resources and people who were eager to assist me.


Fast forward to early-2022, and I've completed a variety of good major and minor projects in which I've put what I've learned into practical use, as well as some freelance work for clients both online and in person. The journey has been amazing and I look forward to keep pushing myself to new heights.
